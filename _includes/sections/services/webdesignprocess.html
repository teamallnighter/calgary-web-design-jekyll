Our Process

Initial Meeting

We will meet with you online to go over your needs for your new site. We will let you know what we need from you to build your new custom site. We will provide you with a list of assets we will need to get started on the project.

Wireframing

We will build out a wireframe with some potential ides for your new site and send them for your review. The wire frame will include place holder images and text

First Revision Meeting

We will meet with you once again (online) to discuss the wireframe ideas and drill down your design needs for the site. This will give us a better idea of what you imagine

Receive Assets

At this point we require your assets. This is normally the number one slow down on any custom website build so we will remind you at the initial revision meetings

The Main Build

With the assets received we will build out the site with your custom content. We will have your branding, logo, copy and images.

Final Revision Meeting

once the main build is complete, We will meet once again to go over any final changes to the site you may require.

Testing

We will test your sites looks, speed and reliability on our test servers before deployment. We make sure everything works and looks great before we go live.

Deployment

The site is live! We will deploy your site to our servers (or yours) and make sure everything is working great with some final tests.
